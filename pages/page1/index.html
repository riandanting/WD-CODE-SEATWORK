WHAT IS ARKANA?
WHAT IS ARKANA?
Arkana is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful heroes face off to protect each other's worlds. Choose from different heroes to make epic plays, secure kills, and defend our worlds.
DESTROY THE BASE
DESTROY THE BASE
The Arcane is the heart of both team's bases. Destroy the enemy's Arcane first to win the game.
YOUR ARCANE
YOUR ARCANE
Your Arcane is where minions spawn. Behind your Arcane is
the Fountain of Lucille, where you can quickly replenish your health and mana
and access the Shop.
ENEMY ARCANE
ENEMY ARCANE
Located in the enemy team's base, the enemy Arcane is just like yours.
Destroying the enemy Arcane wins your team the game.
CLEAR THE PATH
CLEAR THE PATH
Your team needs to clear at least one lane to get to the heart of the enemy Arcane. Blocking your path are defense buildings called towers and inhibitors. Each lane has three towers and one inhibitor.
TOWERS
TOWERS
Towers deal damage to enemy minions and champions,
and provide limited vision from the Darkness of War for their team.
Attack these buildings with minions ahead of you to avoid damage and charge ahead.
INHIBITOR
INHIBITOR
Each Inhibitor is protected by a Tower.
When destroyed, mutated minions will spawn in that lane for several minutes.
Afterward, the Inhibitor will respawn and mutated minions will stop spawning.
FIGHT THE JUNGLE
FIGHT THE JUNGLE
In between the lanes is The Jungle, where monsters and jungle plants thrive. The two most important monsters are Oroboros, the Serpent and Hydra, the Dragons. Killing these monsters grants unique buffs for your team and can also turn the tide of the battle.
OROBOROS, THE SERPENT
OROBOROS, THE SERPENT
Oroboros, the Serpent, is the most powerful creature in The Jungle.
Killing Oroboros grant the slayer's team bonus attack damage,
ability power, and greatly increases the power of nearby minions.
HYDRA, THE DRAGONS
HYDRA, THE DRAGONS
Hydra, the Dragons, are powerful monsters
that grant unique bonuses depending on the element of the dragon your team slays.
There are five Elemental Dragons.
